About Me

I’m Jorge Vargas, an aspiring Data Analyst with a passion for turning data into clear, actionable insights. I specialize in using Python and libraries like Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to explore and visualize data in meaningful ways.

My goal is to transition fully into a Python Data Analyst role — combining my technical background with my passion for meaningful, data-driven insights.
